## 📖 Game Narrative Storyboard

**The Fall of the Moon:**
Once upon a time, the Deity of the Moon governed all lunar affairs, and the citizens of the Moon lived in absolute freedom and peace. However, this prolonged comfort bred complacency. A trusted subordinate orchestrated a sudden underground rebellion. Caught completely off guard, the Deity was easily overthrown. The usurper established a tyrannical regime where the upper class hoarded wealth and power, leaving the lower-class citizens to suffer in misery.

**The Journey Back:**
Stripped of power, the exiled King wandered across various planets, desperately searching for a way home. By a stroke of fate, he discovered a "Ladder to Heaven" on Earth—a hidden pathway leading back to the Moon. To save his people, the King begins his arduous ascent. 

**Level Progression & Lore:**
* **Level 1: The Ascent (Earth to Space)**
  * As the King scales the ladder, he traverses through different atmospheric stages. Along the way, he collects power-ups and fragments of his past.
  * **Memory Fragments:** Reaching specific milestones triggers memory sequences—reminders of the peaceful world he once built and the current suffering of his people. These memories fuel his resolve to reclaim the throne.
  * **Atmospheric Shift:** The journey begins under normal Earth gravity. As the King climbs higher into space and approaches the Moon, the environment shifts, introducing anti-gravity mechanics that change how movement feels and operates.

* **Level 2: The Retake (Lunar Surface & Palace Defenses)**
  * **The Climb:** The King reaches the lunar surface and must scale the palace defenses. This requires intense focus—players must carefully calculate and charge their jumps to navigate treacherous vertical platforms while fighting off the tyrant's guards.
  * **The Final Confrontation:** At the summit, the King faces the tyrannical boss. Rallying behind their true King, the oppressed citizens rise up in support. In an epic final showdown, the tyrant is defeated.

**Epilogue:**
With the throne reclaimed, peace is restored. Having learned from the rebellion, the King no longer settles for mere comfort. He initiates a new era of holistic development, heavily investing in military defense, education, and technology. Decades later, the Moon achieves unprecedented prosperity. When humans from Earth eventually attempt to explore the Moon, the King activates a planetary cloaking mechanism—revealing exactly why Earthlings have never found the lunar civilization.

## 🎮 Gameplay & Mechanics

* **Genre:** 2D/3D Action Platformer 
* **Core Mechanics:** * **Gravity Transitions:** Movement physics dynamically change based on altitude, shifting from standard Earth gravity to floaty, low-gravity/anti-gravity states as the player approaches the Moon.
  * **Precision Vertical Platforming:** In the second phase, navigation becomes highly technical. Players must hold down the jump key to charge their jump power. Miscalculating a jump or hitting an obstacle carries a heavy penalty, as the player risks falling back down to lower platforms and losing progress.
  * **Combat & Collection:** Defeating guards, dodging environmental hazards, and collecting memory fragments that act as buffs to enhance the player's abilities.
